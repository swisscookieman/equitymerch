About EquityMerch

EquityMerch is a passion project, driven by a small team of engaged uni students. Our project came from the simple realisation that many tight-knit communities and associations were unable to provide their members with qualitative and wearable merch, even though demand was present.

We aim to provide Clubs, Communities and Associations with the best possible merch with custom designs, cherry-picked furnishers and easy delivery. Your team wants to explore creating merch? Contact us now for a FREE quote and design offer (limited time offer!).
